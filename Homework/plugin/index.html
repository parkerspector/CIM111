Madrid is considered one of Europes most progressive, modern, and beautiful cities. It has a vibrant local arts culture and boisterous nightlife, along with constant reminders of the cities rich history. It is deservedly renowned as one of the continents best tourists destinations.
My name is Parker Spector-Salwen and I love to travel. These are a few photos from my trip with my family to Madrid.

Favorite Memory

In Madrid, my favorite memory was seeing the 80 sculptures of Las Meninas around the city. Everytime my mom would see a Las Menias, she would take a picture, even if it meant having to cross the street to get it. Her goal was to see how many she could get a picture of, in the hopes to find almost all 80. It was just nice to see my mom get all happy over something as little as a statue.
