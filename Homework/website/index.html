About Me

Currently, I am a sophomore at the University of Miami majoring in creative advertising and double minoring in marketing and interactive media. One of my favorite hobbies is to travel with my family. Most recently, we went to Scotland, Spain and Colombia. Traveling with my family makes going to all these places so much better because I get to enjoy it with the people I love.
